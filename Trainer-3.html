Rohit Khatri Trainer

Rohit Khatri is a well-known Indian bodybuilder, fitness trainer, and social media influencer.

He has won several bodybuilding competitions, including Mr. India 2018 and Sheru Classic 2018.

Rohit Khatri has a huge following on social media platforms like YouTube and Instagram, where he shares his workout routines, nutrition plans, and motivational content.

He is known for his muscular physique and his dedication towards bodybuilding.

Apart from bodybuilding, Rohit Khatri is also a certified personal trainer and helps his clients achieve their fitness goals through personalized workout and nutrition plans.

He has collaborated with various fitness and supplement brands in India and is also the co-founder of the supplement brand 'Muscletech India'.

Rohit Khatri promotes natural bodybuilding and believes in training hard, eating clean, and staying consistent to achieve a fit and healthy body.

He continues to inspire and motivate his followers to lead a healthy and active lifestyle through his fitness journey and knowledge.
