Modern Equipments

Treadmills- These machines allow you to simulate running or walking indoors, making them perfect for cardio workouts.

Ellipticals - Similar to treadmills, ellipticals also offer a low-impact cardio workout, but with less strain on your joints.

Rowing Machines - These machines provide a full-body workout, targeting your arms, legs, and core muscles.

Weight Machines - Modern weight machines can provide a range of exercises targeting different muscle groups, allowing you to build strength and muscle.

Cable Machines - These machines use cables and pulleys to create resistance, offering a range of exercises that can target different muscle groups.

Kettlebells - These weights come in various sizes and shapes and can be used for a range of exercises targeting multiple muscle groups, including swings, cleans, and presses.
