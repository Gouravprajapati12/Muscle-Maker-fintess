Weightlifting

Warm-up: Before starting your weight lifting workout, warm-up for at least 10 minutes with some light cardio or stretching to prepare your muscles for the workout.

Start with a low weight: Start with a low weight: Begin with a weight that is comfortable and allows you to perform the exercise with proper form. Gradually increase the weight as you become more comfortable and stronger.

proper form: It is important to use proper form when lifting weights to avoid injury and ensure that you are targeting the right muscles. If you are unsure of the proper form for a particular exercise, seek guidance from a certified personal trainer.

a variety of exercises: Incorporate a variety of exercises that target different muscle groups for a well-rounded workout. This can include exercises such as bench press, squats, deadlifts, and bicep curls.

between sets: Allow for rest time between sets to allow your muscles to recover and avoid overexertion.Cool-down: After completing your weight lifting workout, cool-down for at least 5-10 minutes with some light cardio or stretching to help your muscles recover.
