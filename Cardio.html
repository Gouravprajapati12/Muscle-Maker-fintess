CARDIO

Treadmill running or walking: This is a popular cardio exercise that can be done on a treadmill, which is a machine that allows you to walk or run on a moving belt. You can adjust the speed and incline to make the workout more challenging.

Elliptical machine: This machine simulates the motion of walking, running, or climbing stairs, but with less impact on your joints. You can adjust the resistance and incline to make the workout more challenging.

Stationary bike: This machine allows you to cycle indoors, providing a low-impact cardio workout. You can adjust the resistance to make the workout more challenging.

Stair climber: This machine simulates the motion of climbing stairs and provides a challenging cardio workout that targets your lower body muscles.

Rowing machine: This machine provides a full-body workout that targets your arms, legs, and core muscles. It simulates the motion of rowing a boat and can be adjusted to provide a more challenging workout.
